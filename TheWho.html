The Who

Formed in 1964

The Who is one of the greatest rock bands of all time. If you've ever enjoyed the opening music to any CSI series, you're a fan of The Who!


The members of The Who are:

  • Roger Daltry, lead singer and guitar
  • Pete Townsend, vocals and guitar
  • John Entwistle, bass guitar
  • Keith Moon, drums

The Who's top singles include:

  1. Baba O'Riley
  2. Won't Get Fooled Again
  3. My Generation
  4. Love Reign O'er Me
  5. Behind Blue Eyes
